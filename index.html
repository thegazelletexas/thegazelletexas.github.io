Re-investing back into communities

By optimizing grazing patterns and livestock populations to prevent overgrazing, ranchers both increase the productivity of their land while avoiding the risk of overgrazing. Gazelle rewards communities and individual ranchers for doing their part through their contributions to the increase in C02 sequestered on their pastures.

Increasing natural biodiversity and C02 Offsets

The protection of grassland cover in Africa's Kalahari is key to protecting valuable wildlife and native species such as Springbok, Gemsbok, Wildebeest, and more. This protection of natural ecosystems not only maintains the ecological balance of the region but maintains healthy populations of cattle and game-species for sustainable ranching and hunting.
